Problem of the Day

SMC posts daily competitive math PoTD's (Problem of the Day's), and all of these are based on important competition math topics. We try to vary the difficulty of the problems for all the varying skill levels in our group. After solving, we have discussion channels to discuss the problems and understand the different strategies of approaching the same answer. These dicussion channels can only be accessed after joining our server.

PoTD Rules:

  • PoTD's are released every day at 4:00 PM PST and remain available to solve until 4:00 PM on the next day.
  • You may not discuss the current problem with others before solving it.
  • You have 5 attempts to solve the problem.
  • Each incorrect answer is -1 points, and correct answers are +5 points.
  • Direct Message "Sacramento POTD Bot" to submit your answer.
  • PoTD Leaderboard can be found below.
