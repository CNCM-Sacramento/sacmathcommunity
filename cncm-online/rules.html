Rules

  • Contest
    • SARML is Sacramento Math Community's mock ARML, or American Regions Math League.
    • The competition is individual, with problems being presented in pairs, with each pair being completed in 10 minutes.
    • Problem difficulty ranges from AMC to olympiad level
    • The only resources allowed are writing utensils, paper, a compass, and a ruler
    • We reserve the right to disqualify any competitor suspected of cheating from the contest
  • Scoring
    • Each answer is an integer
    • Submissions will be graded live, and a live leaderboard will organize all of the scores in decreasing order
